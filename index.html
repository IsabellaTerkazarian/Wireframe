Blankets are cozy, soft coverings used to provide warmth and comfort. They come in a variety of materials such as fleece, cotton, wool, and microfiber, each offering different levels of softness and insulation. Blankets are commonly used on beds, couches, or during travel to keep warm, and they also add a decorative touch to a room. Whether it's a weighted blanket for relaxation or a lightweight throw for cool evenings, blankets are a comforting essential in any home.
